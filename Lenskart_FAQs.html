Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Lenskart?

Lenskart is an online eyewear and eyecare platform that offers a wide range of eyeglasses, sunglasses, contact lenses, and eyewear accessories. They provide customers with the convenience of buying eyewear online and offer various services related to eye health.

2. How can I buy eyeglasses and sunglasses on Lenskart?

You can purchase eyeglasses and sunglasses on Lenskart's website or mobile app. Simply browse through their extensive collection, select the frame and lenses of your choice, and proceed to checkout.

3. What types of eyewear does Lenskart offer?

Lenskart offers a diverse range of eyewear, including prescription eyeglasses, sunglasses, reading glasses, computer glasses, and even safety glasses. They also provide a variety of frame styles and materials.

4. Can I get prescription lenses from Lenskart?

Yes, Lenskart provides prescription lenses. You can upload your prescription or schedule an eye check-up through their home eye check-up service to get the correct prescription.

5. How can I choose the right frame for my face shape?

Lenskart offers a virtual try-on service that helps you try on frames virtually, and they also provide style guides to help you choose frames that suit your face shape.

6. What are Lenskart's virtual try-on services?

Lenskart's virtual try-on allows you to see how frames look on your face through a virtual mirror on their website or app, helping you make the right choice.

7. Are there any discounts or offers available on Lenskart?

Lenskart frequently offers discounts, promotions, and special offers. Check their website for current deals and discounts.

8. How do I place an order on Lenskart?

To place an order, select the eyewear you want, customize it if necessary, and add it to your cart. Then, proceed to checkout and follow the steps to provide your shipping and payment information.

9. What payment options are available on Lenskart?

Lenskart accepts various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, digital wallets, and cash on delivery in some areas.

10. What is Lenskart Gold membership, and what are its benefits?

Lenskart Gold is a membership program that offers benefits like early access to sales, free shipping, and more. You can find detailed information on their website.

11. How does Lenskart's 14-Day Return Policy work?

Lenskart's 14-Day Return Policy allows you to return your eyewear within 14 days of receiving it if you're not satisfied. You can request a return by contacting their customer support, and they will guide you through the process.

12. Can I track my order on Lenskart's website?

Yes, you can easily track your order on Lenskart's website by logging into your account and navigating to the order tracking section. You'll receive updates on the status of your order.

13. What is the Lenskart First Frame Free offer?

The Lenskart First Frame Free offer allows you to get your first frame for free when you buy lenses. This is a limited-time promotion, so check the website for the latest details.

14. Can I get contact lenses on Lenskart?

Yes, Lenskart offers a variety of contact lenses, including daily, monthly, and colored lenses. You can order them online and have them delivered to your doorstep.

15. How can I find Lenskart retail stores near me?

You can use the Lenskart store locator on their website to find retail stores near your location. They have a widespread presence across India.

16. What is Lenskart's home eye check-up service?

Lenskart offers a home eye check-up service where an optometrist visits your home to perform an eye test, ensuring you get the right prescription for your eyewear.

17. How can I redeem Lenskart gift vouchers?

To redeem Lenskart gift vouchers, select your eyewear and proceed to the payment page. There, you'll find an option to apply your gift voucher or coupon code.

18. Does Lenskart offer a warranty on its eyewear?

Lenskart provides a one-year warranty on its eyeglasses and sunglasses against manufacturing defects. This warranty ensures the quality of your purchase.

19. Can I exchange my old eyeglasses for a new pair on Lenskart?

Yes, Lenskart offers an exchange program where you can trade in your old eyeglasses for a discount on new ones. This helps you stay stylish and updated.

20. What are Lenskart's Lens Packages, and how do they work?

Lenskart offers Lens Packages that include lenses with various coatings and features. You can choose the package that suits your needs and enhance your eyewear experience.
